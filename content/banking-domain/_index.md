## Overview

Banking domain is summarized into key categories such as:

* Retail Banking
* Corporate / Commercial Banking
* Private Banking
* Investment Banking
* Treasury and Risk Management
* Compliance & Regulation
* Customer Lifecycle Management

In this series, we will first start with **Retail Banking** and as we move forward, we will cover the rest of the categories as well.
## Key components in Retail Banking:

* ### Accounts
	* Checking and Savings Account (check diff)
	* Savings and Current Accounts
	* Term Deposits (FD/RD)
	* Fixed and Recurring deposits
	* Specific account features - Bonus incentives, Promotional interest rate, ATM facilty, Online banking
* ### Loans and Lending
	* Mortgages: Specific mortgage processes mentioned are Mortgage pre approval, Loan application, Loan processing, Underwriting, and Closing. Further details Mortgage credit management including Data Entry, Indexing, Assessments, Underwriting, Servicing, Rate Switches, and Redemption.
	* Customer Lending - Includes Credit Cards, Automobile Loans, Housing finance, Cross selling
	* General loans - Home, Auto, Personal, Education loans
	* Loan and Credit Management processes - Pre-qualification, Application, processing, document verification, Credit reports, Underwriting and closing
	* Loan recovery processes are also covered, such as Loan credited by bank, Reminder for loan installment, Unable to pay back bank loan, Bank repossess property, and Repossessed property is auctioned/sold
	* Credit risk assessment at the loan origination point involves looking at Character, Management ability, and Environmental conditions and factors
	* Types of loans (Guarantor etc) + Types of proprietorship - sole etc and hows its important in the context of loans
* ### Cards
	* Credit Cards
	* Debit Cards
	* Accessing issued forex card
	* Fraud related to cards, including payment card fraud, card-not-present fraud, card-present fraud, and remote purchase fraud, is a significant concern in the banking sector.
* ### Wealth Management
	* Financial Planning
	* Personal retail banking services
	* Estate plannig
	* Investment Management services
	* Legal and Tax advise
* ### Channels:
	* Branch banking
	* ATM Services
	* Mobile banking
	* Internet banking
* ### Retail finance is listed as a sub-category
	* Customer Onboarding & KYC (Know Your Customer) is part of Retail Banking. Customer Lifecycle Management in Financial Services includes Introduction to products, Awareness, Acquisition, Engagement, Know Your Customer (KYC for the customers), Ease of access to customers, Access to legacy systems, Profiling of customers, Evaluation, Growth of customer base, Data Warehouse, Cultivation of loyalty among customers, Bonding, and Customer relationship (Product and Support Experience)

	* Private banking is listed under Banking and covers Banking for HNWI (High Net-Worth Individuals), Investments for HNWI, and Tax management for HNWI.  Private banking services including Deposit taking and payments, Discretionary asset management, Asset Allocation and managing, Safeguarding and investing, Limited tax advisory services, Advice and investment, and Brokerage.

* ### ML Use cases in Retail banking
	* Cross sell / Up sell models
	* Customer segmentation
	* Personalized offers
	* Scorecards (Application / Behavioural / Collections) - Including Reject inferencing (RI)
	* Fraud Detection (Application Fraud / Payment Fraud / Money Mule Detection) - using techniques like Velocity rules and behavioral profiling.
	* Reccomendation systems
	* Intelligent Chatbots / IVR (interactive voice response)
	* Analyzing customer lifecycle stages (Lead, Apply, Approve, Use, Repay, Renew/Churn) for DS use-cases like Churn Prediction, Lifetime Value Estimation, and Upsell/Cross-sell Pathways
